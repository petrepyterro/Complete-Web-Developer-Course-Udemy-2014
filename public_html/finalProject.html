A few facts about the world we live in

Earth is the third planet from the Sun and is the largest of the terestrial planets. Unlike the other planets in the solar system that are names after classic deities the Earth's name come from the Anglo-Saxon word erda wich means ground or soil. The Earth was formed approximately 4.54 billion years ago and is the only known planet to support life.
